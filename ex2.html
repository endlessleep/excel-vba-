SECTION 3: MASTERING THE [GUESS]

How Excel finds them

=IRR(range; [guess])
  • 1st IRR: Choose a guess close to the first sign-change interval. Excel converges to the root in that region.
  • 2nd IRR: Choose a guess close to the second sign-change interval. Excel converges to the root located in that region.
// Rule:

The guess does not determine the IRR value. It determines which mathematical root Excel converges to, based on the region where NPV changes sign.
