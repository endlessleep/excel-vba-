SECTION 3: MASTERING THE [GUESS]

How Excel finds them

=IRR(range; [guess])
  • 1st IRR: Guess small (0 or 0.05) → Searches the left region.
  • 2nd IRR: Guess large (0.5 or 1.0) → Searches the right region.
// Rule of thumb:

The guess just tells Excel where to start looking on the curve.
